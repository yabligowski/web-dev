My Favorite Cupcakes

  1. Prep

    • heat oven to 350 degrees
    • make sure bowls/measuring cups are clean
    • put liners in molds
    • buy vanilla pillsbury frosting
    • any chocolate mix from store
    • sugar sprinkles

  2. Ingredients

    • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
    • cupcake mix
    • 2 eggs
    • 1/4 cup water

  3. Steps

    1. put your water, vegetable oil, and eggs into a bowl
    2. mix on medium speed for 2 minutes
    3. mix in cupcake mix and continue to mix for 1 more minute
    4. put into cupcake molds
    5. let them bake in oven for 11 minutes
    6. let them cool for 20 minutes

  4. Decoration

    1. frost or pipe on vanilla frosting
    2. coat the cupcakes in sprinkles
    3. let them set in the fridge

  5. Advisory

    1. let cupcakes sit for 1-2 days for the ultimate treat
